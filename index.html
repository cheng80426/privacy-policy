個性計算機 - 隱私政策

MyStyleCalc - Privacy Policy

最後更新時間：2026 年 3 月

Last Updated: March 2026

1. 概述

1. Overview

個性計算機（以下簡稱「本應用程式」）致力於保護您的隱私。本隱私政策說明我們如何收集、使用和保護您的資訊。

MyStyleCalc (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. 我們收集的資訊

2. Information Collection

個性計算機 本身不會收集任何個人資訊。應用程式的所有數據處理都在您的設備上進行。

MyStyleCalc itself does not collect any personal information. All data processing of the App is performed on your device.

2.1 Google AdMob 廣告

2.1 Google AdMob Ads

本應用程式使用 Google AdMob 顯示廣告。Google AdMob 可能會收集以下資訊：

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect the following information:

  • 設備標識符（IDFA）
  • 廣告追蹤資訊
  • 粗略位置資訊
  • Device identifiers (IDFA)
  • Advertising tracking information
  • Coarse location information

詳細資訊請參閱 Google 隱私政策。

For more information, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

2.2 App 追蹤透明度（ATT）

2.2 App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

本應用程式會要求您授予「追蹤」權限，以向 Google AdMob 提供個性化廣告。您可以在應用程式設定中隨時更改此權限。

The App will request "Tracking" permission to provide personalized ads via Google AdMob. You can change this permission at any time in the app settings.

2.3 應用程式內購買

2.3 In-App Purchases

贊助功能透過 Apple App Store 處理。Apple 會處理您的付款資訊。我們不會存儲任何付款資訊。詳細資訊請參閱 Apple 隱私政策。

Sponsorship features are handled through the Apple App Store. Apple processes your payment information. We do not store any payment information. For more information, please refer to the Apple Privacy Policy.

3. 我們不會做什麼

3. What We Don't Do

  • ❌ 不會收集您的個人資訊（姓名、電子郵件、電話號碼等）
  • ❌ 不會建立用戶帳戶
  • ❌ 不會存儲您的計算歷史記錄或任何應用程式數據到遠程伺服器
  • ❌ 不會與第三方分享您的個人資訊（除非法律要求）
  • ❌ Does NOT collect your personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • ❌ Does NOT create user accounts
  • ❌ Does NOT store your calculation history or any app data on remote servers
  • ❌ Does NOT share your personal information with third parties (unless required by law)

4. 資訊安全

4. Information Security

我們採取合理措施保護您的資訊安全。由於 個性計算機 不收集或存儲個人資訊，您的隱私已經得到最大程度的保護。

We take reasonable measures to protect your information security. Since MyStyleCalc does not collect or store personal information, your privacy is protected to the greatest extent possible.

5. 兒童隱私

5. Children's Privacy

個性計算機 適合所有年齡的用戶。我們不會故意向兒童收集個人資訊。

MyStyleCalc is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. 政策變更

6. Policy Changes

如我們對本隱私政策進行重大變更，我們將在應用程式中通知您，或透過其他合理的方式告知您。

If we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you through the app or other reasonable means.
